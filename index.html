Think Before You Trust

AI Evaluation Checklist

Ask yourself these questions every time you read AI-generated text.

1
Does the text actually answer my question?
AI often writes a lot without saying much. Did you get what you asked for?
2
Does the information seem accurate?
If something sounds too specific or surprising — look it up.
3
Is the text too general or vague?
Could this have been written about any country, topic, or situation?
4
Does the AI mention any sources?
No sources = no way to check. Be extra sceptical.
5
Is the text just generic filler?
Swap the country or topic name — does the text still make sense? Then it's not specific enough.
6
Does it show real cultural understanding?
AI has no lived experience. Does the text feel like it was written by someone who knows this place?
7
Are any claims exaggerated or wrong?
AI can "hallucinate" — stating made-up facts with total confidence.
8
Would you hand this in without checking it first?
If the answer is yes — stop and think again. AI is a starting point, not a final answer.

Remember: AI is a tool, not a teacher. Your brain is the quality control.
